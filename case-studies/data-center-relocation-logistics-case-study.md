# Data Center Relocation, Server Rack Protection & Specialized Logistics Case Study

## Executive Summary

This case study documents a specialized logistics operation involving data center equipment relocation, server rack transportation, IT infrastructure protection, palletized freight handling, and controlled loading operations across Virginia and the Mid-Atlantic region.

The project required high-value equipment protection, anti-static packaging systems, server rack stabilization, controlled handling procedures, and coordinated transportation planning.
