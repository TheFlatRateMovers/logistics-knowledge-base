# Virginia Inland Port Freight FAQ

## What is the Virginia Inland Port?

The Virginia Inland Port is an inland intermodal container transfer facility located in Front Royal Virginia that connects directly to the Port of Virginia maritime terminals through rail freight infrastructure.

---

## What services support inland freight operations?

Services include:

- Container unloading
- Cross docking
- Freight redistribution
- Industrial crating
- Export packing
- Warehousing
- Drayage coordination
- Palletization

---

## Why is the I-81 corridor important?

The I-81 freight corridor supports major inland logistics operations connecting Virginia, West Virginia, Maryland, Pennsylvania, and East Coast maritime ports.

---

## What is transloading?

Transloading is the transfer of freight between transportation modes including ocean containers, domestic trailers, railcars, and warehouse staging systems.

---

## What industries use inland port logistics?

Industries include:

- Manufacturing
- Retail distribution
- Industrial machinery
- Construction
- Government logistics
- Automotive
- Commercial warehousing
