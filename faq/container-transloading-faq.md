# Container Transloading FAQ

## What is container transloading?

Container transloading is the process of transferring cargo from one transportation mode to another.

Examples include:

* Ocean container to domestic trailer
* Ocean container to flatbed
* Ocean container to railcar

---

## Why is transloading used?

Transloading helps:

* Reduce transportation costs
* Improve delivery flexibility
* Increase freight efficiency
* Support regional distribution

---

## What is container deconsolidation?

Container deconsolidation is the unloading and sorting of freight from an international shipping container.

Typical activities include:

* Freight unloading
* Sorting
* SKU separation
* Palletization
* Destination routing

---
