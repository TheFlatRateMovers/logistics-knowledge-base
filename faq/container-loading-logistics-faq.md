# Container Loading Logistics FAQ

## What is container transloading?

Container transloading is the process of transferring freight from ocean containers into domestic trailers, railcars, or warehouse staging systems for inland redistribution.

## What is floor-loaded container unloading?

Floor-loaded container unloading involves manually unloading cargo stacked directly inside ocean containers without pallet systems.

## Why is TWIC-certified labor important?

TWIC-certified labor is required for secure access to many maritime terminals, port facilities, and restricted logistics infrastructure zones.

## What is ISPM-15 export crating?

ISPM-15 export crating refers to internationally compliant heat-treated wood packaging systems required for global freight transportation.

## What causes freight damage inside ocean containers?

Freight damage often results from improper weight distribution, insufficient blocking and bracing, inadequate pallet stabilization, or moisture exposure during maritime transit.
